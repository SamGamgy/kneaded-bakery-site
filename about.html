Our Story

Kneaded Bakery began as a weekend ritual of baking sourdough for family and friends. Over time our passion for simple, wholesome food blossomed into a micro‑bakery nestled in Chesapeake, VA. We prepare each loaf, cookie and muffin by hand in our home kitchen in small batches to ensure quality and freshness.

As a Virginia cottage food operation, we are allowed to make and sell low‑risk, shelf‑stable baked goods that don’t require time or temperature control. The exemption permits us to advertise online but prohibits taking orders or payments through this website. That is why you’ll find photos, descriptions and price lists here, but no checkout—just a friendly invitation to get in touch to arrange your pickup.

We label every item with our name, address, processing date and the required notice “NOT FOR RESALE – PROCESSED AND PREPARED WITHOUT STATE INSPECTION.” This disclosure lets you know our products are produced in a home kitchen exempt from routine state inspections. We also repeat the statement on this website in keeping with Virginia Cottage Food Law guidance.

Beyond baking, we believe in community and connection. Each loaf and pastry we share is a small gesture of warmth and hospitality. We hope you taste that love in every bite.

These products are homemade and not subject to state inspection. Sold under Virginia Cottage Food Laws.
